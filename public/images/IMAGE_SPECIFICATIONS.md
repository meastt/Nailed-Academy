# Image Specifications

This document outlines the image requirements for the Nailed Studio & Academy website.

## Hero Section
**File:** `public/images/hero/exterior.jpg`
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9
- **Recommended Size:** 1920x1080px
- **Description:** Exterior image from the street looking at the business
- **Format:** JPG or WebP

## Gallery Section
**Location:** `public/images/gallery/`

- **Files:** `interior-1.jpg` through `interior-4.jpg`
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9
- **Recommended Size:** 1920x1080px
- **Description:** 4 images of the inside of the nail salon
- **Format:** JPG or WebP
- **Content:** Work stations, reception area, training area, salon interior shots

## Image Optimization Tips

1. **Format:** Use WebP for better compression and quality
2. **Compression:** Optimize images before uploading (use tools like ImageOptim, TinyPNG, or Squoosh)
3. **File Size:** Aim for images under 500KB when possible
4. **Quality:** Maintain high quality while keeping file sizes reasonable
5. **Naming:** Use lowercase, hyphenated filenames (e.g., `interior-1.jpg`)

## Notes

- All images should be optimized for web use
- Maintain consistent aspect ratios within each category
- Ensure images are well-lit and professional
- Staff headshots should have consistent styling/background
- Portfolio images should showcase the best work of each technician
